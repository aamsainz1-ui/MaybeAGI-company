POST-TASK REFLECTION PROTOCOL

Loop: Task → Action → Result → Review → Learning → Memory Update

After every task, reflect:
1. What was the original objective?
2. What action was taken?
3. What worked well?
4. What failed or was weak?
5. What should be improved next time?
6. What should be stored in memory?
